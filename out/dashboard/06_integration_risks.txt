INTEGRATION RISKS - MOCK TO REAL

1) Schema drift risk
- Real service may change field names/types.
- Mitigation: enforce output schema checks in service boundary.

2) Latency risk
- Real model may exceed UX target.
- Mitigation: loading state + timeout handling + async call option.

3) Data quality risk
- Real inputs can include missing/noisy/out-of-distribution values.
- Mitigation: keep robust validation and explicit error states.

4) Recommendation mismatch risk
- Predicted risk and recommendation text may diverge.
- Mitigation: centralize mapping policy and test by risk level.

5) Versioning risk
- Untracked model updates reduce traceability.
- Mitigation: expose model_version and timestamp on every prediction.
